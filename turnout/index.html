If it should turn out to be true @ La Panacée, Montpellier, France


This project was developed by Silvia Kolbowski and was commissioned by La Panacée, Montpellier, France, for the exhibition A Letter Always Arrives at its Destinations, curated by Sebastién Pluot. Two short video loops play on adjacent large video screens or room projections.

I designed and developed the video loops using code under the guidance of Silvia Kolbowski. For the final execution of this work, I developed an interactive workflow that allowed Silvia to orchestrate the visual ditortions in real time.
